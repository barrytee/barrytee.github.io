About Pinnacle Tutors Consultancy

 

About Pinnacle Tutors Section Founded on the principles of friendly relationships between Tutors and Tutees, Pinnacle Excel Consultancy aims to help children find the best educational help in their studies. More often than not, the Tutor is left to his own devices to ensure his student's success but we go a little further to help improve the Tutor's standards so as to give the parent a piece of mind.

Pinnacle Tutor Consultancy also look out for forte in Tutors to provide skills related lessons like Finance or Music classes for those minor group who require these special-sought-after skills.

If these are not enough, we provide external solutions like sample exam papers that students can work on to improve in areas that they are not that strong in. At Pinnacle Tutor Consultancy, both Tutors and Tutees are trained to be independent and highly savvy, aspects that are focused on to ensure they are ready in the 21st century. Not only that, these skills carried forward can be useful in their future phase of life.
