Terms & Conditions

 

Agency Commission Clause
Agent fee is deducted at 5% when the tutor is not selected for long-term commitment with tutee.
All agency commission at 50 per cent unless specified.
Agent will receive documentation if assignment is lesser commission.
Agent can choose 4 per cent commission for 10 consecutive months but will be considered case–by-case basis.
Pinnacle Tutors reserve all right to decide which Tuition Assignment will be given the lesser commission payment or monthly payment basis.
Agent will only receive 25 per cent commission during Q4 of 2015.

Tutor Agreement Clause
If tutor wishes to decline the assignment as tutor's schedule doesn't permit, they must do so within 8 hr of tuition class commencement, failing which they have to accept the next assignment or else drop in priority in selection for new assignments.
In accepting the next assignment, they are not to bargain for better assignment rate.
Pinnacle Tutors endeavor to match each tutor to their student but does not guarantee the rate or student aptitude towards learning.

Pinnacle Tutors Agency Promise To Parent Clause
We endeavor to find the best tutor with required qualification but we hold no promise that tutor shortlisted will seek the rates parent wants.

Communication Clause
We do not publish any parent or tutor detail and speak to the media about specific tuition assignment, student name, tutor full name all information that resemble specific person, address are purely coincidental and we bear no responsibility for such coincidence.

Payment Clause
All payment must be done within a week without fail.
Pinnacle Tutors reserve the right to demand payment from parent or tutor in small claim tribunal court.

Timesheet Clause
Timesheet will be issued to tutor who request monthly pay with agency support.
The tutor selected must fill in complete info to facilitate payment else delay in payment due to time needed in administrative documentation expected.

No Show Clause
Tutor who do not turn up for tuition assignment will have to pay the agency 50% for that day’s tuition fee.
If we fail in collecting commission from the errant tutor, tutor name will be communicated to other agencies about no show incidence.
Tutor who wish to avoid paying must provide documentary proof as reason of no-show.

Punctuality Clause
Tutors who are late for more than 10 minutes in 2 consecutive lessons will liable to be terminated by parent, unless they can produce document explaining their inability to be punctual.
Pinnacle Tutors will try to negotiate for resumption of tutor’s lesson but its on a best-effort basis and no guarantee given.
For list of important document, please see Document Clause.

Document Clause
List of Document to justified tutors lateness
1. Signed letter from previous parent before lesson end indicating clearly time departed previous tutee house and reason for extension of lesson. Contact number is the main form of identification and NRIC is optional from Previous parent. Letter must be legibly, else rejected.
2. Timeslip from hospital indicating arrival and departure due to medical appointment.
3. Letter from Workplace signed by Human Resource and reason for holding up.
4. Letter from Court for Court Hearing, which tutor is the witness.
